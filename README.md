## For Reviewers

This is an early draft of a relationship guide my wife and I are building together — based on principles we've developed and practiced in our own marriage. We're sharing it for honest feedback before we take it further.

**What we're looking for:**

- Does anything feel preachy, repetitive, or tone-deaf?
- Which pointers landed for you? Which ones didn't?
- Is anything missing that you wish we'd covered?
- Would you actually share this with someone? Why or why not?
- Any examples from your own life that you think could strengthen a pointer?

**How to give feedback:**

- Open an [Issue](../../issues) — one per topic is ideal so we can discuss each separately
- Or just message us directly — whatever's easier

**What this is NOT:**

- Not a therapy manual. Not clinical advice. Not a replacement for professional help.
- Just one couple's toolkit that worked for them.
